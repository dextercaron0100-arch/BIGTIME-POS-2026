1. System Overview

BIGTIME POS is a point-of-sale system composed of POS Terminal App, Back-office Dashboard, and Central API/database services.

  • Cashier login and shift handling
  • Item selling and payment capture
  • SI/OR receipt issuance and printing
  • Discounts and adjustments (void/return/refund workflows)
  • Inventory and reporting management
  • Online/offline operational continuity

2. Minimum Operating Requirements

POS Terminal: Windows or Android device, printer connection, and local storage for offline queue.

Back Office: modern browser and network access to API/server.

3. Basic Operating Procedure (Cashier)

  1. Launch BIGTIME POS terminal application.
  2. Confirm indicator: Network Ready (online) or Offline Mode (offline).
  3. Enter employee code and PIN to log in.
  4. Open shift (if prompted).
  5. Scan/search items and build transaction.
  6. Apply discounts where applicable.
  7. Select payment method and confirm payment.
  8. Print/reprint receipt as needed.
  9. Perform end-of-shift procedures and generate X/Z reading.

4. Online and Offline Operations

Online mode: Transactions are sent to server services in real time and records update immediately.

Offline mode: Transactions are queued locally and synced after reconnection.

5. Daily Operations and Reports

  • X-Reading (cashier accountability)
  • Z-Reading (end-of-day report)
  • Sales summaries and discount breakdown reports
  • Adjustment-related transaction documents (void/return/refund)

6. Safety, Backup, and Continuity Notes

  • Keep user credentials confidential.
  • Restrict terminal usage to authorized users.
  • Ensure regular backup of server data and report exports.
  • Follow SOP for power interruption and network outage handling.

7. Troubleshooting Quick Guide

  1. Cannot log in: verify credentials and server/network availability.
  2. Printer not printing: check power, connection, paper, and retry print.
  3. Network unavailable: continue in offline mode and monitor sync after reconnect.

8. Document Purpose

This operating manual is prepared as the Item 12 attachment for Annex B submission and serves as a practical usage reference for BIGTIME POS operation.
